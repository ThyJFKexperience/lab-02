## How to Use

Once the application is running you will see 2 buttons and a text field at the top
as well as the current list of cities pre-installed on the application.

To Add Cities;
- Type the name of the city you'd like to add into the provided 'City Name' textbox
- Press the 'Add City' button to add that city to the list

To Remove Cities;
- Click on a city to select it, while there is no visible indication, your selection is saved until you press another city
- After selecting the city by clicking on it, press the 'Delete City' button to delete that city from the list
